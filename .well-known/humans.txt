/* MISSION */
Making web accessibility a search engine ranking factor

Following the HTTPS playbook:
1. User-facing tools create demand
2. Free infrastructure removes barriers
3. Public accountability creates pressure
4. Search engines integrate into rankings

Goal: 50% reduction in accessibility errors on top 1M sites within 3 years

/* STATISTICS */
96% of top websites have accessibility errors (WebAIM 2024)
1+ billion people worldwide have disabilities
$13 trillion in disability community disposable income
